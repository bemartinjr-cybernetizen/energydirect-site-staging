How choosing a Houston plan works

Who does what

CenterPoint Energy owns the poles, wires, and meters and restores outages, no matter which plan you pick. Your retail plan only sets your price, term, and billing, which is the part we help you get right.

Pricing varies by ZIP and usage from The Heights and Katy to Pearland and Sugar Land, so a smart comparison starts with your address, not a single "Houston average."

What we check on every plan

  • Price at 500 / 1,000 / 2,000 kWh — matched to your home size.
  • Minimum-usage fees — charges if you use too little.
  • Bill credits — the usage level where they kick in.
  • Term & early-termination fee — what leaving early costs.
  • Renewable content — the green percentage, if it matters to you.

Houston electricity questions

Will my lights go out when I switch providers?

No. CenterPoint keeps delivering power without interruption while your new plan simply begins billing.

Who is the utility (TDU) in Houston?

CenterPoint Energy is the Transmission and Distribution Utility for most of the Houston area. They manage the lines, outages, and delivery regardless of your retail plan.
