Houston electricity questions

Quick answers

How do I compare electricity rates in Houston?

Houston is in a deregulated part of Texas, so you choose your retail electricity plan while CenterPoint Energy delivers the power over the same wires. Enter your ZIP code to see the plans available at your address, then compare the price per kWh at 500, 1,000, and 2,000 kWh, plus the contract term and any fees, before you enroll.

Will my power get interrupted if I switch?

No. Switching only changes who bills you for energy. CenterPoint Energy still delivers the electricity and handles outages, so there is no interruption and no new wiring.

Are there no-deposit electricity plans in Houston?

Yes. No-deposit plans are available for many Houston customers, subject to a quick eligibility check. Enter your ZIP to see which no-deposit options you qualify for.
